SwitchHero Privacy Policy


This app does not collect personal information or any personal data.


Settings and game information are saved locally to your device. This app does not require any additional permissions from your device to function.


Any activities performed on Nintendo.com is done through your phone's Safari browser and no personal details/information are shared back to the app or stored locally. Any purchases made through the Nintendo.com website are entirely contained within the web browser and functions the same as if you have accessed the site through your browser. Cookies and any transactions made through Nintendo.com are subject to Nintendo's privacy policy.
